Section 1:

Shoujo / Slice of Life

Out of all the genres, I've watched the most series under the romance / slice of life category. Something about high school romance comedies are just pleasing and entertaining for a chill night of anime. A few of my current top favorites under this genre would be, "Fruits Basket" and "Kaguya Sama Love is War".
